# Privacy and Safety

## Health data principles

- Collect the minimum data needed.
- Keep adults in control of their own sharing.
- Treat children as guardian-managed profiles.
- Encrypt data in transit and at rest when backend sync is added.
- Maintain audit logs for shared records.
- Allow export and deletion.
- Never sell health data.

## Consent model

- `owner`: the admin's own profile.
- `guardian-managed`: child profile controlled by guardian.
- `pending`: adult invited but not accepted.
- `accepted`: adult accepted sharing.
- `revoked`: sharing disabled.

## Medical safety

The app can:

- track symptoms and medicines
- show reminders
- summarize records
- show trends
- recommend contacting a clinician

The app must not:

- diagnose disease
- tell users to start/stop medicine
- override emergency care
- promise that a symptom is safe
